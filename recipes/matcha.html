Matcha Beverage

Earthy and Energizing

Ingredients

  • tablespoon of matcha powder
  • cup of milk
  • two tablespoons of water
  • sweetner of choice

Steps

  1. Take a tablespoon of matcha powder and mix it in the water
  2. Take the matcha mixture and pour in milk and mix again
  3. Add sweetner if desired
  4. Drink up!
