Cacao Beverage

Savory and Healthy

Ingredients

  • tablespoon of cacao powder
  • cup of milk
  • two tablespoons of water
  • sweetner of choice

Steps

  1. Take the tablespoon of cacao powder and mix it in the water
  2. Take the matcha mixture and pour in the milk and mix again
  3. Add sweetner if desired
  4. Drink up!
